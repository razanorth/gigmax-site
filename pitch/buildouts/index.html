The Problem

Everybody knows organic UGC works.
Getting it off the ground is a nightmare.

01

Creators can be unreliable.

Hundreds of cold DMs. Marketplaces that promise "vetted" talent. Creators continue to disappear before they post or mid-campaign.

02

The ops can bury your team.

Contracts, payments, briefs, live tracking, and account warming. And someone still has to come up with the actual content ideas.

03

3-6 months to build internally.

Heavy logistics for new teams makes proving out this channel slow, so it gets deprioritized. Your competitors end up eating up the mindshare in your niche while you're still figuring out the process.

04

Agencies cost $45K to $90K/quarter.

And when the contract ends, you don't own the creator relationships or infrastructure. You have to restart the channel from zero.
